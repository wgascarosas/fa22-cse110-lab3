Agenda

In this meeting we covered the details involving the first project. For the first project we brainstormed some potential ideas, narrowing our focus down to a new social media application. This new social media app would take a picture of you (selfie) in real time as well as a picture of whatever is behind your back facing camera. Each day a notifcation would be sent out to remind users to take their time sensitive photo. The idea is to capture the real activities one may do on a day to day basis. Essentially to connect with others on a more "real" level versus a surface level one that msot other social media apps are used for.
